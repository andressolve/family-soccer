Spring Family Soccer

Our 13-Week Training Plan
Andres · Maria · Francisco · Sebastian
Saturdays · April – June 2026
April May June

April

Weeks 1–4 · Foundations

Getting comfortable with the ball. We learn how to control it, move with it, pass it, and receive it. The building blocks for everything else.

Week 1 Saturday, April 4

⚽ Ball Mastery

  • Learn to keep the ball close using toe taps and sole rolls
  • Get comfortable touching the ball with every part of your foot
  • Challenge: how many juggles can you do? We'll count your personal best!
Watch before Saturday: Toe taps technique
Bonus video: Sole roll mastery exercises
Week 2 Saturday, April 11

💃 Dribbling Basics

  • Dribble with the inside and outside of your foot — keep the ball glued to you
  • Head up while dribbling — peek at what's around you!
  • Shadow dribble: one person leads, the other copies every move
Watch before Saturday: Laces dribbling technique
Week 3 Saturday, April 18

🤝 Passing

  • Use the inside of your foot for accurate passes — like a hockey stick!
  • Lock your ankle and point your toe up for a firm, clean pass
  • Pass through cone gates — how many can you hit in 2 minutes?
Watch before Saturday: Inside-foot passing
Week 4 Saturday, April 25

🧮 First Touch

  • Soft feet! Cushion the ball when it comes to you — like catching an egg
  • Receive, turn, and go — control the ball with your back to the passer
  • Bounce game: control the ball before it bounces twice
Watch before Saturday: 5 essential first touch drills

May

Weeks 5–9 · Skills & Tactics

Time to level up. Shooting, tricks, goalkeeping for Sebastian, defending, and how to play together as a team.

Week 5 Saturday, May 2

💥 Shooting & Goalkeeping GK Week

  • Shoot with your laces for power — plant your foot and follow through
  • Target practice: hit the cones in the corners of the goal
  • Goalkeeping intro: ready position, "W" hands, and basic saves (Sebastian's time to shine!)
Watch before Saturday: Shooting technique — striking for power
Week 6 Saturday, May 9

✨ 1v1 Moves

  • Step-over: swing your foot over the ball, push away with the outside
  • Scissors: circle the ball, then go the opposite way — fake them out!
  • Body feint: drop your shoulder one way, go the other — sell the fake!
Watch before Saturday: 1v1 moves — stepover, scissors, and more
Week 7 Saturday, May 16

🔀 Change of Direction

  • Inside cut and outside hook — change direction without losing the ball
  • The Cruyff turn: fake a shot, drag the ball behind you — like the pros!
  • Cone gauntlet: zig-zag using a different turn at each cone
Watch before Saturday: Five turns to escape pressure
Week 8 Saturday, May 23

🥅 Goalkeeping Deep Dive Sebastian's Session

  • Diving saves: start on your knees, build up to standing — be brave!
  • Catching technique: "W" hands for high balls, scoop for low balls
  • GK footwork: shuffle along the line, stay balanced, never cross your feet
  • 1v1 saves: come out, make yourself BIG, and narrow the angle
Watch before Saturday: Goalkeeper catching and handling
Week 9 Saturday, May 30

🛡 Defending

  • Stay on your feet! Don't dive in — be patient and wait for the right moment
  • Jockeying: keep your body between the attacker and the goal
  • Sebastian as GK: learn to shout directions to your defenders — be the boss!
Watch before Saturday: 1v1 defending drill

June

Weeks 10–13 · Game Time

Put it all together! Team play, real game situations, and a family tournament to celebrate everything we've learned.

Week 10 Saturday, June 6

👥 Combination Play

  • The wall pass (give-and-go): pass, sprint, receive — the ultimate team move!
  • Overlap runs: pass wide, then run around your teammate for a return ball
  • 2v1 attacks: use the extra player to create an advantage
Watch before Saturday: Wall pass and combination play
Week 11 Saturday, June 13

🎮 Small-Sided Games

  • Rondos: keep the ball away from the defender — quick thinking!
  • 2v2 mini tournament: first to 3 goals wins — game on!
  • Game awareness: "freeze!" — where should you be? What are your options?
Watch before Saturday: Fun small-sided soccer game
Week 12 Saturday, June 20

🎯 Set Pieces & Game Situations

  • Corner kicks: deliver the ball into the box and attack it!
  • Free kicks: pick your spot and aim for the corners
  • Decision time: should you pass or dribble? Read the game!
No video this week — we'll practice these together on the field!
Week 13 Saturday, June 27

🏆 Family Tournament!

  • Skills challenge: dribble relay, shooting accuracy, juggling record
  • GK save challenge: how many can Sebastian stop?
  • Mini tournament: parents vs. kids, mixed teams, 1v1 and 2v2
  • Awards ceremony: best goal, best save, best move, most improved!
Get pumped: Fun rondo game to warm up for tournament day
